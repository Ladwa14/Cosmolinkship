OUR SERVICES

Cosmolinkships is one of the most dynamic firms in the maritime industry, driven by a dedicated in-house team of qualified professionals and a strong network of trusted global partners. Our commitment to discipline, teamwork, and the latest technology ensures precision and consistency in everything we do.

Through our local and international teams, we manage every operation with care and efficiency — delivering prompt, cost-effective, and competitive solutions that strengthen the performance of our clients worldwide.

Cosmolinkships is an expert in the fields of Ship Management, Trading & Broking, Open Registries, and Technical Consultancy. With extensive experience and a global network, we provide professional maritime solutions that meet the highest industry standards.

What We Do

Freight Trading

Cosmolinkships delivers independent dry-freight trading for industrial clients, miners and traders—negotiating and administering spot, COA and time-charter contracts with speed and rigor.

Commodity Broking

Cosmolinkships provides tailor-made physical broking that helps principals optimize and expand trading under FAS/FOB, FOB-ASV, CIF/C&F and Ex-Ship terms.

Technical Services

Our marine engineers and naval architects deliver inspections, repairs oversight and technical guidance that lift safety, reliability and environmental performance.

Surveys

Cosmolinkships’ surveyors cover all vessel types—hull & machinery, pre/post-entry, damage/loss, repairs supervision, casualty, draft & inclining, class and IMO compliance.

Studies

Naval-architecture studies: ship design/mods, operating manuals, trim & stability, grain/damage stability, tonnage-load line/freeboard and longitudinal strength calculations.

Advisory & Compliance

High-quality consultancy for ship managers—audits and guidance to comply with ISM, FSA, ISPS, IMDG, MARPOL 73/78, ISO 9001 and ISO 14001.
