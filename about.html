About Us

Cosmolinkship, is an expert in the field of open registries and technical consultancy. The firm has deep roots in the industry, dating back to 1968, when The Makris family, also founders of Honduras Maritime Services (HMS), commenced providing services for ships hoisting the Honduran flag.

Today Cosmolinkship provides comprehensive administrative and technical services to the maritime industry.

Our Goal

At Cosmolinkships, we deliver reliable, end-to-end shipping solutions—owning every task from kickoff to completion. We remove operational burden for clients and work to the highest international standards, earning long-term trust worldwide.Α long list of satisfied international customers is testament of the trust and confidence that our clients have.
